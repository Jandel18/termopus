## 🧰 Troubleshooting

If you run into problems, try these steps:

- Make sure your Windows firewall allows termopus through.

- Check your internet connection on both your phone and computer.

- Confirm that you have the latest version on both devices.

- Restart termopus on your computer and phone.

- Make sure your phone and computer are on the same network or that your computer allows remote connections over the internet.
